Oven Roast Chicken with Vegetables

Description

A very easy to make recipe that is delicious and filling that can be prepped in less than 15 minutes

This recipe is flexible enough so that you can do it to suit your own taste

Ingredients

  • 2lb of chicken (use the parts that you like the most or even an entire chicken)
  • Salt & garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Chopped Vegetables like potatoes, carrots, sweet potatos, eggplant, onions, you name it
    • |
  • Seasonins

Steps

  1. Put all your chopped up vegetables in a large baking dish and place your chicken on top of your vegetables
  2. Sprinkle salt and garlic powder over the chicken and vegetablees, be genrous.
  3. Use mexican, italian or bbq seasonings to make the recipe different each time
  4. Sprinkle vegetables and chicken with olive oil
    5. |
  5. Put it in the oven and wait until the chicken is golden and vegetables are done.
